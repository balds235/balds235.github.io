1C2S Engine Development

My senior capstone challenges me to master CAD software, study two-stroke engine design, and build a transparent demonstration engine. The final model will cycle through both strokes reliably while making every internal motion visible with smoke trails.

Project Overview

Driving Question
How can I learn CAD to model a one-cylinder two-stroke engine and 3D-print a non-firing demonstration model to show how it works?
Project Summary
I am researching two-stroke configurations, sketching concepts, developing CAD models, 3D-printing iterative prototypes, and refining the assembly until it can run through both strokes smoothly without breaking.
Final Deliverable
A polished, transparent engine model with visible internals that can be cycled by hand while smoke traces the airflow.
Project Intentions
Build core CAD proficiency, deepen my understanding of engine mechanics, and practice design iteration and rapid prototyping.

  • Career Ready Practice #1: Apply technical skills and academic knowledge.

Goal: Develop a functioning layout for the 1C2S engine.

  • Positioned intake/exhaust ports, crank geometry, and piston travel to support full-cycle motion.

Weekly Snapshot
