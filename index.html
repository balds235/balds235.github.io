Featured Senior Capstone: 1C2S Engine Development

For my senior capstone I am designing and 3D-printing a one-cylinder, two-stroke demonstration engine. The project chronicles my journey into CAD, engine research, prototyping, and teaching others how two-stroke powerplants operate.

  • Driving Question: How can I learn CAD to model a one-cylinder two-stroke engine and 3D-print a non-firing demonstration model?
  • What I'm Building: A transparent, smoke-ready model that clearly shows both strokes and the internal airflow.
  • Why It Matters: The work strengthens my mechanical engineering fundamentals—CAD, design iteration, and engine theory—while creating a teaching tool for my peers.
