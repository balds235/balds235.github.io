Genesis (GAB)

1:1 In the beginning God created heaven and earth.

1:2 The earth was empty, and hollow darkness covered the face of the earth; the Holy Spirit moved the far over waters.

1:3 And God spoke: Light be made. And light was true.

1:4 The light was seen by God, and it was good: and he divided the light from the darkness.

1:5 He named the light day, and dark night; there was dusk and dawn the very next day.

1:6 And God spoke: Let there be a support in the sea, let it divide the waters.

1:7 And the support was created by God, dividing the waters under the support from the ones above, and it was so.

1:8 God called the arid lands, Earth; and the congregation of waters, the sea. God saw it to be good.

1:9 It was said by God: Congregate all the waters underneath heaven in one place: and let the arid land appear. And it was true.
