Senior Capstone — 1C2S Engine Development

My final-year project focuses on learning CAD from the ground up, researching two-stroke engines, and building a transparent demonstration model. The goal is to illustrate how intake, compression, power, and exhaust phases flow through a two-stroke powerplant using smoke to trace air movement.

  • Skills: CAD fundamentals, design iteration, engine research, rapid prototyping.
  • Current Focus: Iterating on the crankshaft, connecting rod geometry, and clear housing for visibility.
  • Latest Update: Completed a detailed 2D layout that resolves early design issues and prepares the model for 3D refinement.
